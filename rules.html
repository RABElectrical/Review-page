£100 Amazon Voucher Draw: Rules

How to Enter

To enter the prize draw, a current or past customer of R.A.B Electrical must leave a review. If a customer is to refer a new customer, both the referring customer and the new customer will receive and extra 2 entries into the draw after the new customer's job has been completed and paid for in full.

There is NO LIMIT to the number of people you can refer. The more you refer, the more entries you get!

Eligibility

The prize draw is open to all R.A.B Electrical customers (current, past, or new) who meet the entry criteria. Employees of R.A.B Electrical and their immediate family members are not eligible to enter.

Prize

The prize is a single £50 Amazon e-gift card. The prize is non-transferable and no cash alternative is available.

Draw Dates

The next prize draw will be held on the 20th December 2025 - just in time for Christmas! All eligible entries up to the 17th December 2025 will be included in the draw. The winner will be chosen at random and notified by email or phone within 2 days of the draw date.

Consent & Data

By entering the draw, participants consent to their name being announced publicly as the winner. Personal data will be used solely for the purpose of administering the prize draw and will not be shared with third parties.

Contact

For any questions regarding these rules, please contact us directly via email.
